Who am I?

    My name is Roque Antonio Nido Alvarez, I am a senior graduating from the New Jersey Institute of Technology in May 2022. I am majoring in Biology while representing my institution as a NCAA Division 1 athlete for the men's volleyball team at NJIT. Additionally, I am part of the Puerto Rico Men's Volleyball National Team and compete internationally every year. My goals are to become a practicing physician while also expanding my athletic career. This webpage serves to present both my academic and athletic profiles.
